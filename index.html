How it works

Frankstell's car pre‑ordering service in five clear stages.

1

Reach Out

Tell us the make, model, trim, year, and your budget. If the budget is unrealistic, we suggest a realistic target. If it’s fixed, we recommend cars within it.

2

Selection Stage

We send links to vehicles that match your brief from Copart and reputable US dealerships. After you choose, we share a clear total‑cost range to get the car to you.

3

Commitment

Once a price is agreed, you pay a commitment fee of 10% of the total cost or $1,000—whichever is higher. This counts toward the car payment and confirms readiness to complete purchase after a win.

4

Post Bidding

If we win the auction, you settle the car in full. Frankstell pays the auction house and prepares the vehicle for shipment.

5

Shipping

Shipping typically takes 6–8 weeks to Tin Can Island, Lagos, Nigeria. You can clear the car yourself or use our reliable, fairly‑priced clearing agents. Delivery support available.
